💡 HOW TO USE:

1. Find the video you want to download
2. Press F12 in your browser (Developer Tools)
3. Go to Network tab
4. Filter by "m3u8"
5. Play the video
6. Copy the m3u8 URL that appears
7. Paste it in the downloader
8. Select quality
9. Click Download!
